Our Services

From routine maintenance to complex removals, our qualified team handles every job with care, skill and professionalism.

Tree Removal

Safe, controlled felling and complete removal of hazardous, diseased or unwanted trees of any size, leaving your site clean and tidy.

Crown Work

Crown reduction, lifting, thinning and shaping to improve tree health, reduce wind resistance and enhance light and views around your property.

Stump Grinding

Complete stump removal using specialist grinding equipment, eliminating trip hazards and regrowth, and restoring the area for replanting or landscaping.

Hedge Trimming

Expert cutting and shaping of hedges of all heights and species, keeping boundaries neat, healthy and well-maintained throughout the year.

Why Choose Foxstone?

We're a family-run team of qualified tree surgeons passionate about keeping trees — and the people around them — safe. Whether you need emergency work on a dangerous tree or a scheduled programme of maintenance, we bring the same level of care and expertise to every job.

Our team holds NPTC/LANTRA qualifications and we carry full public liability insurance, so you can have complete peace of mind from the moment we arrive to the moment we leave your site clean and tidy.
